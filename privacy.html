Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

PriceMoji ("we", "our", or "us") operates the PriceMoji mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App. We are committed to protecting your privacy regardless of where you are located.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Account information (email address, display name) if you create an account
  • Shopping lists and price alerts you create
  • Community deals you post
  • Support requests and feedback

Information collected automatically:

  • Device information (device type, operating system version)
  • App usage data (features used, session duration)
  • Approximate location (city-level, only if you grant permission, used to show nearby store prices)
  • Camera access (only when you actively scan a barcode or product, no images are stored on our servers)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide price comparison and barcode scanning features
  • To send price drop alerts you have opted into
  • To display community deals relevant to your area
  • To improve the App's performance and features
  • To respond to your support requests
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, we process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Consent: Where you have given us explicit consent (e.g., location access, notifications)
  • Contract: Where processing is necessary to provide the services you requested (e.g., account features, subscriptions)
  • Legitimate interest: Where processing is necessary for our legitimate business interests (e.g., improving the App, preventing fraud), balanced against your rights
  • Legal obligation: Where we are required to process data by law

4. Data Storage and Security

Most of your data (shopping lists, scan history, preferences) is stored locally on your device. If you create an account, your account data is stored on secure servers with encryption in transit and at rest.

We implement industry-standard security measures including TLS encryption, access controls, and regular security audits. Our servers may be located in various regions; by using the App, you consent to the transfer of data to these locations, subject to appropriate safeguards.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Hosting, analytics, and payment providers who process data on our behalf under strict data processing agreements
  • Legal requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect our legal rights
  • Community features: Deals you post publicly are visible to other users (your display name only, never your email)

6. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. When we transfer data internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission, or other legally recognised transfer mechanisms.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Restriction: Request that we limit how we use your data
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent: Withdraw consent for optional data collection at any time (location, notifications)

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@pricemoji.com. We will respond within 30 days (or sooner where required by local law).

8. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by applicable law.

Local data (stored on your device) is deleted when you uninstall the App.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age (or 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

10. Third-Party Services

The App may contain links to third-party websites (retailer sites). This Privacy Policy does not apply to those sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies. Third-party services we use include:

  • RevenueCat: Subscription management (processes purchase data)
  • Google/Apple: In-app purchase processing
  • Telegram: Bot integration for price alerts (only if you opt in)

11. Region-Specific Disclosures

European Economic Area, UK, and Switzerland (GDPR): You have the rights described in Section 7 above. You may also lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority if you believe your rights have been violated.

California, USA (CCPA/CPRA): California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, request deletion, and opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information. To make a request, email support@pricemoji.com.

Brazil (LGPD): Brazilian users have similar rights to access, correct, delete, and port their data. You may contact us or Brazil's National Data Protection Authority (ANPD) with concerns.

Other jurisdictions: We respect the privacy laws of all countries where our users are located. If your local law grants you additional rights not listed here, please contact us and we will honour them to the extent required by law.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or via email. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices:

  • Email: support@pricemoji.com
  • Website: pricemoji.com

For data protection inquiries specifically, you may also reach us at admin@pricemoji.com.
